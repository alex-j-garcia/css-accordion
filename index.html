Coronavirus - Facts, advice, and measures

Facts and knowledge about COVID-19

  • Common symptoms of COVID-19 include a fever, cough, and shortness of breath
  • Symptoms occur 2 to 14 days after being exposed to the virus
  • However, some people have no symptoms. And others have severe symptoms. In some cases, COVID-19 can be fatal

For the public

  • Social distance, quarantine, and isolation
  • Hand hygiene, cough etiquette, cleaning and laundry
  • When children have acute respiratory tract infections
  • Risk groups and their relatives
